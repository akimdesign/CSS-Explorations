Discover Hamilton

Whether you are looking for a hiking adventure, good eats or wanting to explore Hamilton's culture, you can check out these adventure maps and plan a trip!

Spend a day(or two!) exploring the lush forests and scenery of the escarpment and don't miss visiting some of Hamilton's 10,000 waterfalls!

Feeling hungry? Wander through downtown Hamilton and seek out popular eateries and coffee shops. Hamilton is also known for its craft brewery scene so don't forget to have a pint!

Wanting to feel culutured? Visit the Art Gallery of Hamilton for works by famous artists or tour down James St and meander into small shops and galleries owned by local artists.

See if your favourite movie stars are hanging out in Hamilton! Visit the areas where The Boys, The Umbrella Academy and more are filmed. Can you spot the scenery while you are watching the shows?
